About Us

Located in the scenic city of Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, QKhalil University is a forward-thinking institution dedicated to academic excellence, research innovation, and global citizenship. Founded with a vision to empower future leaders through transformative education, we blend international standards with deep respect for Central Asian heritage and values.

Mission: To cultivate critical thinkers, ethical leaders, and lifelong learners who contribute meaningfully to local and global communities.
